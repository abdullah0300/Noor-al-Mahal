Easy Steps to Buy Car from Japan Auto Auction

Follow our simple 7-step process to purchase premium Japanese vehicles directly from auctions. Get access to exclusive vehicles with competitive pricing.

REGISTRATION

Please make a simple registration in our system. It is FREE and will allow you to control your deposits, auctions, bids and payment.

Advance Deposit

In order to be able to start bidding and purchasing cars right away, you are required to make a deposit. With deposit in your account, you get a VIP status and can see all cars coming up in the auctions. Most auction cars are only visible to VIP accounts only. 3

Make Bid for your Preferred Car

Choose your required vehicle from our auction stock list and place the Bid using the Make Bid form. We will receive your bid request and will make the bid in the Auction on your behalf.

Professional Bidding Service

Our experts handle all auction processes for you

4

Bidding Results

Successful Bidding

If bid is won, we will send you the invoice. You'll receive detailed information about your successful purchase and next steps.

Failed Bidding

If bid is failed, we will return your deposit or you can use it for the next bid. No additional charges apply.

Final Payment

After we send you the proforma invoice you can arrange the Balance payment within 72 hours.

Payment Deadline: 72 hours from invoice receipt
