About Noor Al Mahala Trading

Your trusted partner in premium Japanese vehicle export for over 15 years. Quality, transparency, and speed are at the heart of everything we do.

Our Story

Founded in 2008, Noor Al Mahala Trading began as a small family business with a simple mission: to provide customers worldwide with access to the finest Japanese vehicles at unbeatable prices.

What started as a local operation in Osaka has grown into a globally recognized name in the Japanese car export industry. We've built our reputation on three core principles: quality vehicles, transparent processes, and lightning-fast service.

Today, we proudly serve customers in over 95 countries, having exported thousands of vehicles and earned the trust of dealerships, businesses, and individual buyers worldwide.

Our Mission & Vision

Driving excellence in Japanese vehicle export

Our Mission

To provide seamless access to premium Japanese vehicles worldwide, ensuring every customer receives exceptional value, quality, and service throughout their purchasing journey. We strive to make car importing simple, transparent, and reliable.

Our Vision

To become the world's most trusted Japanese car export company, setting the industry standard for quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction. We envision a future where premium Japanese vehicles are accessible to everyone, everywhere.
